About Me

My engineering complex was forged in the competitive furnace of high school robotics ( Team 9725: The Clockwork Cardinals ) where I learned code, wire, and hammer. My skills were annealed through college summer internships at Fulfl Solutions and Mesodyne that exposed me to real-world engineering problems in a professional work environment. I quenched my thirst for team work and collaboration with my senior capstone project and various other club projects. Finally, my expertise in mechanical design was ground to a fine polish with my recent work experience at a Romanian robotics startup company. In-between daily chores I squeeze my creative juices into making cool mechatronic projects. Check out my project page for more information!
