Benchtop 2-in-1 Toolpost

When inserting a cutting tool in a 5C collet (used on the Tormach mills), the collet needs to be secured in place while a torque wrench is used to tighten it properly.

The challenge was to design a toolholder to hold 2 different collet sizes as to economize for space on the worktable.
